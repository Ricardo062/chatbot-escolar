School Courses and Programs – Summary:

Professional Courses:

Include practical components and technical modules.

Professional Courses for the 2025/2026 school year: Técnico de Multimídia, Técnico de Apoio Psicossocial, Técnico de Informática - Sistemas, Técnico de Desporto, Técnico em Mecatrônica Automóvel, Técnico de Vendas e Marketing.

Scientific-Humanistic Courses: Ciências e Tecnologias, Línguas e Humanidades, Ciências Socioeconômicas, Artes Visuais.

Students complete the Supervised Internship (FCT).

The courses prepare students for the job market and for higher education.

Supervised Internship (FCT):

Mandatory internship for professional courses.

Usually carried out in the 11th or 12th year of Secondary Education.

The workload varies depending on the course.

Students may receive help from the school to find a company.

Requires a final evaluation report.

Professional Aptitude Test (PAP):

Final project for professional courses.

Includes a written report, a product or practical component, and an oral presentation.

Evaluated by an examination board.

Demonstrates the professional skills the student developed throughout the course.

Course Change:

Students may request a course change at the beginning of the school year.

They must contact the Class Coordinator and the School Office.
