Access to Grades, Absences and School Information – Inovar Consulta

The school uses the platform "Inovar Consulta" to provide students and guardians with access to academic information.

What can be viewed in Inovar Consulta?
- Grades for all subjects
- Absences (justified and unjustified)
- Late arrivals (punctuality records)
- Class schedule and school calendar
- Assignments, tests and evaluations
- Teacher notes and observations
- Important dates and announcements
- School documents uploaded by teachers or direction

Who can access the platform?
- Students
- Parents/Guardians

Each user has an account associated with the school.

How do parents access their child’s grades?
1. Go to the Inovar Consulta page of the school.
2. Enter your login and password (provided by the school or activated through the "Activate Access" option).
3. After logging in, parents can view:
   - All grades for each subject
   - Absences and justification status
   - Punctuality records
   - Test dates and deadlines
   - Documents sent by teachers
   - Important school notifications

How do students access their grades?
Students access the same platform using their school-provided login credentials.

Recovering or activating access:
- If a parent or student forgets their password, they can use the “Recover Password” option on the Inovar page.
- If the account has never been activated, use the “Activate Access” option.
- In case of access issues, the parent or student must contact the Class Director (DT) or the School Secretariat.

What information is required to activate a parent’s account?
The school may request:
- Full name of the student
- Student number
- Parent/guardian email
- Identification document (in some cases)

Security:
- Parents should not share their login with students.
- Students must not attempt to access their guardian accounts.
- Personal information is protected according to school and national privacy regulations.

Platform availability:
- Inovar Consulta is accessible 24/7.
- Access requires internet connection.
- Works on computer and mobile devices.

Notes:
- All official academic records and absence details are updated in Inovar Consulta by teachers and administrative staff.
- This platform is the official source for grades and attendance information.
