School Name: Escola Secundária Dr. Augusto César da Silva Ferreira
Location: Rio Maior, Portugal
Type: Secondary School with Professional Courses
Main Areas: Scientific-Humanistic Courses and Professional Courses

General School Information:
- The school serves students from the 7th to 12th grade.
- It offers both academic and professional pathways.
- It includes a library, study rooms, bar, cafeteria, reprography, and administrative offices.
- The school follows the official Portuguese education guidelines.

Administrative Services:
- The Secretariat is responsible for issuing school documents, certificates, and registrations.
- The Direction of the School coordinates all academic and administrative processes.
- Students may request school documents through the Secretariat.

Contacts:
- Students should contact their Director of Class (DT) for academic issues.
- Professional course students should contact the Course Coordinator.
- For psychological or academic guidance, students may contact the School Psychologist.
